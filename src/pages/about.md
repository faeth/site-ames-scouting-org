Scouting offers unique skills that complement sports and other activities. Scouts develop leadership skills, learn first aid, train outdoor skills, explore community service, and more. Scouts also enjoy adventures outside. Go on a hike, enjoy a campout, paddle the waterways in a canoe, and go on many more adventures.

Scouting works with your schedule. Scouts can advance at their own pace and there are no mandatory meetings.

Scouting is for all youth ages 5 through 17. Children ages 5-10 participate in Cub Scouts and ages 11-17 participate in Scouts BSA.
